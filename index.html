Welcome to Project Future

The Kelley MSIS Program's Project Future

Project Future is an initiative led by the MS in Information Systems Program at the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University. This project aims to support small businesses and NGOs transitioning to online-based operations, a need that became especially critical during the global pandemic.

Teams of student volunteers, guided by their professors and mentors, work directly with these organizations to provide technology-based consulting and solutions. The goal is to streamline the process of client intake, team assignment, and delivery of results, ensuring that both the organizations and the students benefit from the experience.

Through Project Future, students gain hands-on experience in consulting, while organizations receive vital assistance in adapting to the digital age.
